Recent Platform Updates

  • Open Elections: You can now create elections that are open to everyone by enabling the 'Allow anyone to Vote' option. Anyone with a Gmail account can participate without requiring a voter register. Note that for open elections, voter turnout will always display as 100% since no predefined voter register exists.
  • Election Identification: Each election has a unique identifier that can be shared with voters. For non-anonymous elections, the creator's email and name serve as the election's identifier, ensuring transparency since voter details will be visible to the creator.
  • Real-Time Vote Preview: While all core functionalities of the platform remain completely free, election creators can preview voting progress before closure with our premium View feature. This optional functionality is available with a one-time $6 lifetime license, reflecting how real-world elections typically don't reveal interim results until polls close.
  • 10,000 Voter Capacity: Both open and restricted elections maintain the same 10,000 total voter limit.
  • Voter Instructions Template: Access a customizable template with instructions to share with your eligible voters, helping them navigate to and participate in your election.
