Voter Instructions Template

Share these instructions with your eligible voters to guide them through accessing and participating in your election

As an election creator, you can copy and customize the template below to provide clear instructions to your voters. This will help ensure everyone can easily access and participate in your election.

How to Use This Template

  1. Copy the template text below
  2. Replace the highlighted information with your specific election details
  3. Share the completed instructions with your eligible voters via email, messaging platforms, or your organization's communication channels

Dear Voter,

You are invited to participate in the [Your Organization Name] election for [Election Purpose].

To cast your vote, please follow these steps:

1. Visit www.letmyvotecount.com

2. Click the 'Vote Now' button on the homepage

3. You will be asked to authenticate with your Gmail account through Google OAuth 2.0 to ensure vote integrity

4. Under "Vote in Existing Elections," search for our election using the name: [Your Election Name]

5. If our election is restricted, you can verify it using the unique ID: [Election ID] (For non-anonymous elections, look for the creator's email and name instead of an ID)

6. Follow the on-screen instructions to cast your vote

This election will remain open until [End Date and Time].

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact [Contact Person/Email].

Thank you for your participation!

[Your Name/Organization]

Note: If you've created an open election (where anyone with a Gmail account can vote), make sure to mention this in your instructions and omit references to voter registration requirements.
